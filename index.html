About Me

My name is Cîrjeu Daniel Marian, but my friends call me Cioco.
I am an IT enthusiast, always passionate about everything related to PCs. I'm the person who friends always called when they had an issue with their PC, needed an upgrade, or had a PC-related question.
While working as an IT Technician in my last job, I primarily checked the condition of used notebooks, replaced faulty parts as necessary, and completely erased data for refurbishing and sale.
During this time, I realized I could achieve more. I wanted to develop my own skill set that represents me, something I can continuously improve and that challenges my creativity.
So, I decided to pursue programming. In February 2023, I started studying to become an IT Specialist in Application Development. I am still pursuing this goal today, while also completing an internship and learning Web Development.
